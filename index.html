Who is Nike?

$NIKEPIG emerged from a viral tweet by Cardano founder Charles Hoskinson, who shared a story about his pet pig, Nike. The amusing contrast of a pig — typically associated with laziness and gluttony — sharing a name with one of the most iconic sports brands caught the crypto community's attention. Within minutes, $NIKEPIG was created as a tribute, fueled by the community's love for memes.

Charles later revealed Nike's backstory: the pig had been mistreated and shockingly fed bacon by a previous owner. Nike was rescued and given a new life on Hoskinson's ranch, where he's thrived ever since.

The token's market cap soared past $38 million USD in just days, surprising even Charles. $NIKEPIG drew users from Ethereum, Solana, and Bitcoin to Cardano — many setting up wallets and exploring DEXs for the first time, making $NIKEPIG a unique cross-chain onboarding gateway.
